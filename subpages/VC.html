Imperial Decree of Victory Conditions

Switzerland - The Swiss Guard

Use condotierri to sponsor a war in which France eliminates an Italian player. Switzerland may not ally France.

Switzerland is famous for our unique halberd-centric mercenaries. Derivative of this, we have managed to become even more embroiled in Italian politics than the Italians themselves. The French realize our superior military tactics and consistently employ us to facilitate their easterly ambitions. We’re not sure whether it is their coin or their diplomats’ opinions on the Swiss that have advanced our position in the empire…but this is no time to change our method.

Naples - Support Skanderbeg

Share great-power status with a sovereign Albania.

Having wrested the throne from the Angevin monarchs, Alfonso V has secured the Kingdom of Naples for his illegitimate son Ferdinand. Before dying in 1458, he attempted to protect Albania via vassalage; he owed a debt for their aid during his own invasion and was inspired by their string of victories against the waxing Turkish threat. If we are to maintain our position of power we must not shirk from our opportunities to assert it.

Milan - Patron Saint Ambrose

The religious war has concluded and there are no Protestant (including Reformed) nations with the Latin culture group.

Milan embraces Saint Ambrose as its patron Saint. In times of strength we go as far as to consecrate our Republican tradition in his memory. A stalwart defender of Roman Catholic tradition, Ambrose was beleaguered by heretical doctrines spreading during his era. It is obvious how Saint Ambrose would act when faced with the plague that is Protestantism, and we are compelled to act in accordance with his wishes.

Savoy - Break the French Yoke

Exceed the development of France.

Savoy has long been sovereign, yet the courtiers of Europe rarely acknowledge the Duke of Savoy as much more than a steward who prepares the earth it rests on for France’s inevitable occupation. If France were to be weakened substantially we could finally forge a destiny of our own.

Tunis - Axe to Grind

Simultaneously own the entire island of Sicily and the province of Rome.

Tunis, the geographic successor state to Ancient Carthage, is overdue for retribution against the remnants of ancient Rome. Time has eroded the customs and creeds of our ancestors – conjuring new mutual causes for strife. There is bound to be a sadistic pleasure in realizing Hannibal’s ambitions, even if our counterblow comes a thousand years too late.

Florence - Chancellor Niccolo Machiavelli

Unite enough Italian states to create the Kingdom of Italy.

Our diplomats are singing new songs following the unexpected appointment of a previously exiled man as Chancellor…

Genoa - Barbary Crusade

Own all coastal provinces in the Mediterranean Sea with Tunisian cores on them.

Genoa has long aspired to the greatness of Venice. We compete for Mediterranean trade-posts, maintain fleets of galleys to rival Venice’s naval prowess, and we even mimic the Venetian brand of Republican government. How better to overshadow our rival than to do what they have not? Purging Tunis from the map will strengthen us physically, but more importantly…in the eyes of our peers.

Navarra - A Necessary Adaptation

Control eight trade-cities in Mediterranean trade-zones. Each trade-city must have existed for at least ten years.

Navarra is the last bastion of the Basque culture. Kept under lock-and-key by the neighboring juggernauts that curtail any hope of our expansion, we have precious little time to make a pivotal, political venture. If we are willing to set aside our pride as monarchs, we are in the perfect position for colonial and trade enterprise which won't require much expansion. Perhaps our trade-prince rivals have grown complacent during our incarceration?

Bavaria - Rekindling the Empire

Be the reigning Holy Roman Emperor while all nations with capital cities in the Italian region that are north of Rome are also members of the Holy Roman Empire (post 1490) or prevent the Shadow Kingdoms event (ante 1490).

The liberal policy that the Austrian Emperor has proposed thus far regarding the treason in Italy can be summated as a blatant lack of action. Favoring his dangerous obsession on foreign policy, our combined Imperial strength is eroding beneath us. Though we are biased, we are not alone in contemplating the unified stength that would be obtained beneath a Bavarian emperor; many electorates are ready to cast their votes for us, on the condition we use the power to reign Italy back into the Empire.

Venice - Venetian Opportunism

If no other player has achieved their goal by the end of the timeline, then all were undoubtedly thwarted by the machinations of the mischievous Venetian player. Therefore, Venice wins.

Only Venetian cunning could keep a Republic so well preserved in such an age of barbarous monarchism. Founded in 431 A.D. – we’ve borne witness to the fall of western Rome, we’ve outlasted our republican peers in Pisa and Amalfi, and we’ve even enlisted crusaders as mercenaries to maintain our interests. Despite contributing to the collapse of eastern Rome – we ensure our presence in their final hour. We’ll usher the French into Milan when it suits us and we’ll resume our Papal servitude when it doesn’t. The blatant, clumsy emulation displayed by the Genoese flirts with a certain flattery that only encourages our behavior.



Papal Bulls

By divine order of his Holiness,

Obviously, no in-game exploits. Out-of-game exploits (E.g. mailing your political rivals anthrax) are fine.

While games are in session communication is only allowed in Discord. Yes, this operates on the honor code. Publically shame violators.

Discord will be limited to four channels, three of which are faction-exclusive.

Faction exclusive channels are only active while respective faction leaders are sovereign and in control of their historic capital.

Factions cap at four players.

If a faction leader loses all cores permenantly the faction in question is factionless until next session.

New factions will be created to replace factions that have been extinguished.

No breaking character in Discord during sessions. You're a 15th Century Diplomat and you'll like it.

Eliminated players are "Princes in Exile" and will remain in Discord pleading other nations to restore them to their throne.

Ledger is limited. Difficulty is set to Normal. New World is standard. Lucky Nations are enabled.

Do not abuse the cloudshare; load in as your own nation during practice sessions.

Sessions hosted on Tuesdays from 7PM-10PM.

Winner gets a DLC, if you already have them all I will gift you the next one released for EU4.

By divine suggestion of his Holiness,

Eliminated Players should expect to be released in treaties.

All players should anticipate constant land gain and loss.

Underhanded sleights are highly encouraged.

Attend 99% of sessions.

Read three chapters of the menu before each session to get into character.

Read the word document in the cloudshare to understand the spirit of the campaign.

Refrain from meta-gaming, you don't need the DLC prize that badly.

Post concerns (regarding the rules, victory conditions, the website, session scheduling, etc...) on the message board located here.
